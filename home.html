WHY CHOOSE US?

If you own a sedan or smaller SUV, traveling with your dog is about to get much more comfortable-for the dog of course.

The foldable bridge design is made to fit a range of differently sized sedans and SUV's to seat both passengers and your pets! The upper edge has been reinforced for no sagging once it's attached to the headrest. Security straps and "pods" also work to hold the liner in place even while your pet paces back and forth.

The PetMyRide team has developed a coated cover that installs over the entire rear seat of your car so your pet will have plenty of room to sprawl out on long trips.

Our water and tear resistant cover cover allows your dog to walk around and stretch without any slipping and sliding. Not to mention it protects your seats from the unwanted hair and dirt that comes with traveling together. Your pet will be happy and your carpet and back seat will stay clean under the Cover Travel Liner, making it a great solution for dog owners who frequently travel with their pets!
